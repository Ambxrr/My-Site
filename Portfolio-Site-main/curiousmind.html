The Curious Mind of Junji Ito, 2025 5" x 7" Accordion Book, Heavyweight Cardstock, Handbound Cover

The Curious Mind of Junji Ito is an accordion book I created to showcase the work of one of my favorite horror manga artists, Junji Ito. The book explores his unique approach to horror through a series of illustrations and insights into his creative process, along with some information about the artist himself!
